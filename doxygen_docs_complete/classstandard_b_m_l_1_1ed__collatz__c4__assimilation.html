Detailed Description

Uses the equations from Collatz et. al (1992) to calculate the CO2 assimilation rate. Currently only intended for use by Ed.

First, reaction rates are modified from their values at 25 deg. C according to the leaf temperature.

Then, three different CO2 assimilation rates are calculated, corresponding to conditions limited by rubisco, light, and CO2.

Finally, an overall gross assimilation rate is calculated based on these three limits, and net assimilation is found by subtracting leaf respiration.
