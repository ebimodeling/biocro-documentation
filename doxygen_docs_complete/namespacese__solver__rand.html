std::vector< double > random_vector_on_n_dimensional_sphere ( size_t  n)

A function that returns a random vector on the surface of an n-dimensional sphere.

Here each component is chosen from a normal distribution, and then the vector is normalized to the unit sphere. See section 4 of "Three Different Algorithms for Generating Uniformly Distributed Random Points on the N-Sphere" by Jan Poland (Oct 24, 2000). May be available at https://www-alg.ist.hokudai.ac.jp/~jan/randsphere.pdf.
