Detailed Description

Determine the stomata water stress factor.

If the soil water content is below the wilting point, the stress factor takes its minimum value. If the water content is at or above the field capacity, the stress factor reaches its maximum of 1. Between the wilting point and the field capacity, the stress factor is determined by a straight line.
