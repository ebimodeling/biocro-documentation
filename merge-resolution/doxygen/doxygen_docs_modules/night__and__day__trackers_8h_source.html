80 // Update the output quantity list //

83 // The day and night trackers are produced at a light-dependent rate and decay exponentially independent
84 // of the light level
85 // These quantities were inspired by the light-sensitive protein P in
86 // Locke at. al, Journal of Theoretical Biology 234, 383SPA393 (2005)
87 update(night_tracker_op, tracker_rate * ((1.0 - light) - night_tracker));
88 update(day_tracker_op, tracker_rate * (light - day_tracker));
